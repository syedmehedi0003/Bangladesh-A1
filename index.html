Bangladesh 2.0

Idea , innovation , vision and challenges in New Bangladesh 2.0

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0

Innovation

From the earlier “Digital Bangladesh” phase to now a “Smart Bangladesh” vision (with e-governance, 5G, digital services, startups) aimed at 2041

Sustainability

Bangladesh is rapidly expanding solar and wind power — the Solar Home System program has provided clean energy to millions of rural homes.

Infrastructure

The Chittagong Port and Mongla Port are expanding, and the Payra Deep Sea Port project aims to become a key South Asian trade hub.

Empowerment

Programs in agriculture, fisheries, and handicrafts increase rural incomes and community leadership.
